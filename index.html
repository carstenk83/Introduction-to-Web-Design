The Cost of Mentorship in Whiplash

Whiplash is a 2014 drama film written and directed by Damien Chazelle, starring Miles Teller as an
ambitious young drummer and J.K. Simmons as his demanding, abusive mentor. The film explores themes of ambition and
perfection, earning critical acclaim and three Academy Awards, including Best Supporting Actor for Simmons.
